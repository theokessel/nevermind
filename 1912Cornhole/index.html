Intensive Exercise

Cornhole is the aerobic equivalent of playing 7 back to back football games with a fourty pound backpack on your back. Michael Phelps, the most decorated Olympian ever, says he "would not have been able to do the doggy paddle if it wasn't for his offseason cornhole training."
